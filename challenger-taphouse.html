Our Story
More Than
a Bar.

Challenger Tap House is Lancaster's go-to destination for craft beer lovers, food enthusiasts, and sports fans alike. We built this place with one mission: give our community somewhere exceptional to gather, eat, drink, and belong.

From our rotating craft tap list to our kitchen's signature smash burgers and fall-off-the-bone wings, every detail has been crafted with care. Our staff treats every guest like a regular from day one.
